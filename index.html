Private Parties

the banquet hall and a function hall are venues that are rented by an individual, a group, an organization or an authority to hold specialized events. They have a lot of similarities in terms of structure, pricing and usage such that they are often used for the same purpose and are referred to interchangeably. However, conceptually they are very much different.Banquet halls are buildings or portions of buildings that are booked by individuals or groups of individuals for holding private functions like weddings, anniversaries, birthday parties, etc. They are mostly constructed as inseparable parts of a hotel, resort or commercial entertainment hub.Cleaning, maintenance and supervision are key aspects of successful event management. No event or party becomes successful due to its front-office executives. It’s always the back-office staff like the cleaners, waiters, helpers and attendants who make an event successful.

Meetings

Private conference rooms are a more traditional set-up. They are separated from the hustle and bustle of the main office floor to provide employees with a quiet, secure place to work.Offices that don’t offer private rooms for their employees often have issues with space management. Employees may need to take a personal call, or discuss work topics that are only intended for a specific group of workers. This means that they need to fight it out for a quiet corner, swap workspaces, and leave the office floor in chaos.The downfall of private rooms is that they can make collaboration and ideation much more difficult, because they can exclude employees who may be able to contribute to the discussion.
